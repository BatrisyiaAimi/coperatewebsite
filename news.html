Selasa, 16 Jun 2009

Educate underprivileged children

WITH the strong desire to help young children in need of protection, Lauzaei Md. Finally, 42, and his wife Siti Aishah Ghazali, 42, decided to build a charity house to give a ray of hope to its residents. The charity house has been operating since January 2010 at Bandar Country Homes, Rawang, Selangor. Lauzaei, who is also an ustaz, rented a twin house to serve as a shelter for the children in the name of Rumah Amal Budi Bistari.

The main objective of establishing a charity house is to help those in need. It is also used as a center for fardu ain recitation for low school children, youth and the local community. It is also a platform for forging relationships between those who are capable and those who are less fortunate.

The Amal Budi Bistari House accommodates 40 small children aged five to sixteen. In addition to providing dormitories, the charity house is also responsible for providing all the completeness of the basic needs so that it comes to the learning aspect of the children.
